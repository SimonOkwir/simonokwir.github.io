About me

Simon Okwir is a researcher and lecturer at Uppsala University, specializing in technology, operations, and sustainability. He holds a dual Ph.D. in Industrial Management and Economics from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden and Universidad Politécnica de Madrid in Spain. His research explores operations and supply chain strategy, sustainability, and algorithmic technologies. Simon incorporates artistic interventions—such as poetry, podcasting, and symbolic forms of expression into engineering classrooms to foster a sustainability mindset among students. He has collaborated with industry leaders, including SWEDAVIA, AENA, and IBERIA airlines, on the implementation of Airport Collaborative Decision Making (A-CDM) in Sweden and Spain, as well as ICA Supermarket on blockchain feasibility in food supply chains. His current research includes sustainability education, supply chain imaginaries, and supplier carbon transparency.
