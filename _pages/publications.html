Publications

I am interested in operations management, supply chain, circular economy, and the application of artificial intelligence in organizational contexts.

  • Samples of my work on operations management: Journal of Air Transport Management (2017, 2025), Integrated Communications, Navigation and Surveillance Conference (2014).
  • Samples of my work on circular economy and sustainability: Journal of Cleaner Production (2021), Academy of Management Proceedings (2020).
  • Samples of my work on artificial intelligence and innovation: International Journal of Organizational Analysis (2024), Organization Studies (2023).
  • Samples of my work on performance measurement: International Journal of Management Reviews (2018), IEEE Transactions on Engineering Management (2019, 2021).
